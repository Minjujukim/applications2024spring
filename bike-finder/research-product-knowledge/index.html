The Buying Experience

Where to Buy

  • Bike shops - great for face to face advice and can be measured based on your physical size
  • Online - great for many options from multiple retailers but might have to assemble it yourself
  • Used - to save money

Examples questions to ask when buying

  • Do you think it iwll suit the type of riding I want to do?
  • Do you offer a guarantee?
  • what basic checks do you think I should learn for myself?
  • For second hand - When was the last time the chain / chainset / chainring were replaced?
